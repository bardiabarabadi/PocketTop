# App Store Screenshot Copy

Taglines and titles for the marketing screenshots. Layout per frame:

- **Tagline** (small, on top) — one sentence.
- **Title** (massive, 2 lines, 2–3 words per line).
- **Screenshot** below.

Pick one tagline + one title per frame. Alternates are listed for tuning against the device frame.

---

## Notes

- Keep titles to 2 lines, 2–3 words per line — tested to breathe well at 1320 × 2868 (iPhone 6.9").
- Taglines are sentence-case, end with a period.
- If a title looks crowded against the device frame, switch to the shorter alt.
- Open-source is a first-class selling point — keep Frame 7 (or a cover tagline that mentions it) in every shipped set.
